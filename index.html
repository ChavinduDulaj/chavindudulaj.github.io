Heritage Decode

(Currently Working on)

Heritage Decode is a pioneering mobile application project developed by our second-year data science group, in collaboration with the archaeology department, aimed at translating ancient Sri Lankan stone inscriptions. Located in the historic sites of Anuradhapura, these inscriptions hold significant cultural and historical value. Our methodology involves gathering raw data through the archaeology department, employing data cropping and semantic segmentation techniques for data preprocessing, and utilizing a database and comparison algorithm for translation. Integrated into a user-friendly mobile application interface, Heritage Decode offers real-time inscription translation capabilities, bridging the gap between ancient history and modern technology, and promoting cultural preservation and education.
